# GameCo-Sales-Analysis
## **OBJECTIVE**   
The objective of this analysis is to leverage historical video game sales data to generate data-driven insights that support Gamco’s strategic decision-making around game development, market targeting, and sales forecasting. By analyzing global and regional sales trends, genre performance, and platform popularity over time, this project aims to identify market opportunities, challenge outdated assumptions, and provide actionable insights that help GameCo better align its products with consumer demand across different regions.

## **KEY QUESTIONS**   
This analysis was guided by the following key questions:  
1. **What are the overall global video game sales trends over time?**     
   - How have total unit sales evolved from 1980 to 2016?     
2. **How do video game sales differ across geographic regions?**   
   - What roles do North America, Europe, Japan, and other regions play in global sales performance?  
   - Have regional contributions remained consistent, or have they shifted over time?      
3. **Which game genres perform best globally and by region?**  
   - What genres consistently generate the highest unit sales?  
   - How do regional preferences differ by genre?  
4. **Which gaming platforms dominate global sales?**  
   - What platforms have achieved the highest lifetime sales?  
   - How does platform performance influence publisher dominance?    
5. **How has game popularity changed over time?**  
   -  Are certain genres or platforms gaining or losing relevance?  
   - What trends can inform future game development and forecasting? 
6. **What insights can Gamco use to guide strategic planning?**  
   - How can sales trends, regional preferences, and platform performance inform marketing, development, and competitive strategy?

## **DATA** 
**Source:** VGChartz Video Game Sales Dataset  
**Time Period:** 1980 – 2016  
**Metrics:** Units sold (in millions)

**Dataset Overview**
- Tracks global and regional video game unit sales
- Includes sales by genre, platform, publisher, and region
- Regions analyzed:
  - North America
  - Europe
  - Japan
  - Other Markets
Note: Sales figures represent total units sold, not revenue.

## **TOOLS**  
- Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning, aggregation, pivot tables
- Microsoft Word & PowerPoint – Documentation and presentation
- Data Visualization – Bar charts, line charts, and trend analysis.

  ## **METHODOLOGY**
1. Data Cleaning
- Removed empty rows and irrelevant columns
- Addressed missing regional sales values using mean imputation
- Standardized game names and corrected formatting inconsistencies
- Rounded all sales figures to two decimal places
- Validated data accuracy and consistency before analysis.

2. Data Processing
- Aggregated sales data by:
  - Year
  - Region
  - Genre
  - Platform
  - Publisher
- Used pivot tables to summarize performance across markets
- Conducted descriptive statistical analysis to identify trends and outliers

3. Data Analysis & Visualization
- Examined long-term sales trends over time
- Compared regional market contributions
- Analyzed genre popularity globally and regionally
- Identified top-performing platforms and publishers
- Created visualizations to clearly communicate insights and trends
